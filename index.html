Comic-Con

Comic-Con is the largest convention of comics, superheroes, toys, and pop culture around the world. It is a huge event that transforms the entire city. There will be people dressed up in costumes everywhere and many restaurants and bars will have Comic-Con themed events.

Downtown San Diego

La Jolla is about a 20 minute drive from downtown San Diego. The area is filled with tons of things to visit:

  • The Gaslamp is a 16-block area in the heart of downtown. There, you can do some shopping in Horton Plaza, go to a dueling piano bar, or eat at one of the many great restaurants.

Breweries

San Diego is notorious for its craft beer. There are hundreds of breweries scattered around San Diego county, and they are a must-see if you are in the area. Many breweries have games, food trucks, music, and other forms of entertainment. Here are some maps of breweries in the area.
